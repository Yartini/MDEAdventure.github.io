A New Perspective

As you reflect on your journey through the MDE program, you realize the transformative power of design thinking and its integration with mechanical engineering.

The IDE 385 course and the other IDE courses this semester opened your eyes to a more holistic approach to problem-solving, one that considers not only the technical intricacies of mechanical systems but also the human experience, sustainability, and societal impact.

You find joy in the alignment between your interests and the MDE program's focus on interdisciplinary experiences. The decision to switch from mechanical engineering was a pivotal moment, leading you to a major that not only challenges you but also resonates with your passion and abilities.
