Reflecting on Your Path

As you think about the information provided by the MDE advisor, take a moment to reflect on your interests and aspirations. Consider the following questions:

  • What aspects of the Multidisciplinary Engineering program resonate with you?
  • How do the program's goals align with your career and personal objectives?
  • Which concentration(s) within the program intrigue you the most?
  • Do you see yourself thriving in a multidisciplinary and collaborative engineering environment?

Schedule a meeting with your classmates to discuss your reflections.
