A Culmination of Experience

As a senior in the Multidisciplinary Engineering (MDE) program, you reflect on the diverse experiences and challenges that shaped your academic journey.

The program's emphasis on interdisciplinary studies has provided you with a unique perspective, allowing you to integrate knowledge from various engineering disciplines and apply it to real-world problems.

Your senior year is marked by the completion of a comprehensive capstone project that encapsulates the essence of the MDE program. This project brings together elements of acoustical engineering, educational engineering, and engineering management, showcasing the versatility of your MDE education.
